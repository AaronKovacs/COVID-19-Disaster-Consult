About Us

We are a group of Emergency Medicine physicians and Disaster Medicine specialists working to provide easy to use clinical resources for front line healthcare workers. We do our best to review current literature, best practices, and guidelines in order to give you the most relevant and important information. We welcome feedback and constructive criticism.
